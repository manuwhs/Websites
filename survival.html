After the first utopian Madrix crashed, the Architect rebuilt the second Madrix with what he described as "grotesqueries" of human nature. You are the chosen one! To save humankind you must be prepared to overcome the threats of the journey.

The sentinels used the Artificial Intelligence techniques taught in this course to grow more intelligent. They have better weapons and are capable of performing great image and voice recognition, becoming the perfect search-and-destroy units.

Your only chance of survival is to find the Survival Guide. Inside it, you will find all the knowledge you need to succeed in Madrix.

But be careful!! The Sentinels guard this holy document and they will destroy you if they ever have the chance!
