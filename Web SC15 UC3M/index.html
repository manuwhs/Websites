General Description


Do you want to learn about the latest telecommunication technologies?

Would you like to learn what is next to 4G technology?

Do you want to discover how telecommunications is closely related with other fields such as medicine or sports?


Telecommunications are becoming the future of engineering. With technology being able to connect us with practically every place on our planet, we are able to share knowledge with anyone. This course is intended to serve as an introduction to the field of telecommunications and illustrate the potential of presented methods in a range of study applications.
